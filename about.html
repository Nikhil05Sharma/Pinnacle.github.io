About us

We are a young-blooded multifaceted organization filled with immense passion

and a belief that everything is possible with the right amount of determination!

Pinnacle Youth Welfare Society is an NGO founded a group of students, mainly Vaibhav Bhardwaj and Nikhil Sharma. They have a vision to serve people across the country, which has encouraged him to undertake various initiatives to help people of all stature, including providing them with state-of-the-art resources. Pinnacle Youth Welfare Society has helped thousands across the world and is making constant efforts to raise awareness, provide care for those in need, and encourage many more to join us in serving humanity.

Your smallest contribution makes a big difference to person’s lives. We count on the generosity of people like you to be able to create real change for India’s people!

Mission and Vision

Our mission is to transform the lives of the disadvantaged by empowering them with essential tools required to lead a healthy and productive life. Through these measures, we strive to create a progressive community and encourage individuals to look beyond their economic limitations. Through our work, we wish to ignite a spirit of altruism and a sense of humanism amongst citizens.

Our vision is to lay down a path for the underprivileged by aiding them to access better quality education, provide career opportunities, and support a functioning healthcare system. We take our efforts with the aim of helping these individuals to attain self-reliance

Our Approach

Our approach is modeled around bringing change at all levels.

With your support,we address children’s critical needs by working with parents, teachers, Anganwadi workers, communities, district and state level governments as well as the children themselves.

We focus on changing behaviours and practices at the grassroots level and influencing public policy at a systemic level – thereby creating an ecosystem where children are made the nation’s priority.
