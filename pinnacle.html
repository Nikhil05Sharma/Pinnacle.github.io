What we are?

Pinnacle Youth Welfare Society (PYWS) is a non-governmental organisation whose aim was to serve the people at the topmost level.We are the PYWS family and wanted to make a world which is secure and full of happiness for everyone and gives a system as well as a platform to work together for the betterment of our people as well as the world. We can take collective efforts to improve our social and living conditions.We wanted to become a helping hand for persons who needs our support and also to work for their well beings. Our oranisation wanted to help everyone including poor children by providing educational support, helps to empower women's, distributing food to poor's, etc and also work for the cleanliness and hygiene in our locaclity to make our city clean and green.

We acts as a mediator between government and citizen. When few issues that the does not reach to government or are not solved by the government then our society have to play a significant role in assigning these issues to the government and few issues are intentionally looked by our NGO. Our main aim is to make the earth a better place for every human being who is suffering. The function of PYWS is to focus on all the issues concerning human rights, social, environmental and advocacy. VThe work to promote and improve the social and political conditions of the society on a broad scale.
